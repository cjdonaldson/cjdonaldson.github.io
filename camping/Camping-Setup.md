# clock time:
- press and hold the clock button
- enter time in 24 hour format
- press clocl button again to save

# Auto-level Nulling
- manual level the RV
- power off the leveler
- depress and hold auto-level
- press and release power button
- wait for 5 - 6 beeps
- release auto-level button
- (null is now set)

# Tank cleaning
= [The Truth About RV Black & Gray Tanks | How to Prevent Clogs, Odors & Sensor Problems](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ALZgId6X2Ng)
- fresh water tank
    - fill with water
    - 16oz of bleach
    - let sit for 24 hours
    - drain and flush with fresh water
- gray water tank
    - fill with water
    - 16oz of Dawn Ultra
    - let sit for 24 hours
    - drain and flush with fresh water
    - (perform this last night before leaving - dump on exit)
- black water tank
    - fill with water
    - 1oz of RV Digest-It+
    - let let it work for 24+ hours
    - flush
